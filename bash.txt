Bash
----

[ctrl+k]:Cut to the end of line (kill)
[ctrl+y]:Yank (paste)
[ctrl+c]:Kill process (cancel)
[ctrl+z]:Pause process
[ctrl+s]:Pause output
[ctrl+q]:Resume output
[ctrl+x]>[ctrl+e]:Edit command
[ctrl+r]:Search history
[ctrl+d]:Hangup
[alt+t]:Swap the last two words
[alt+u]:Uppercase next word
[alt+l]:Lowercase next word
[ctrl+e]:Move cursor to end of line
[ctrl+a]:Move cursor to begining of line
[ctrl+g]:Leave the history search mode
[ctrl+l]:Clear
[ctrl+w]:Cut previous word

Tmux
----

[ctrl+b]>[%]:Split pane horizontally
[ctrl+b]>["]:Split pane vertically
[ctrl+b]>[{]:Move pane left
[ctrl+b]>[}]:Move pane right
[ctrl+b]>[z]:Toggle pane zoom
[ctrl+b]>[[]:Enter copy mode
